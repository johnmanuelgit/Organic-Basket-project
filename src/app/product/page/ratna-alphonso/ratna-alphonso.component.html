Ratna alphonso mango is a gigantic fruit, each mango weighs around 500-750 gms. It is rich in taste and It has a really small mango stone or seed so this results into more pulp. About Ratna Alphonso Mango

Origin

Ratna is a mango variety of Indian origin grown in the state Maharashtra and some parts of Karnataka. It is a hybrid variant made from the Alphonso and Neelam mango. It is considered to be one of the high yielding mango varieties.

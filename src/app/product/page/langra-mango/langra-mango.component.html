About Langra Mango

Origin

Langra mango is said to originate from Varanasi, current Banaras. In the Hindi language, Langra means lame. It got its name from a 300 years old tale which depicts a lame or “langra” farmer who ate these mangoes and sowed its seeds in his farm. Hence, langra mangoes of Banaras have a distinct and best taste. Now, it’s grown all around India.

  • Found in Banaras, Uttar Pradesh.
  • Banaras produce the best langras.
  • Named after a 300 years folk tale.

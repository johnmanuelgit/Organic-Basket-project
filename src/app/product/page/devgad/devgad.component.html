About Devgad Alphonso Mango (Devgad Hapus)

Origin

A Portuguese explorer, Alfonso de Albuquerque, brought Alphonso mangoes to India. From Goa, mangoes traveled to places like Ratnagiri and the southern regions. Growers brought grafts from Ratnagiri to Devgad in the early 19th century and planted them in the uprooted trees of other species. It resulted in the production of Devgad Alphonso mangoes.
